About us

Jona Adult Family Home was established in 2020 with a mission to provide exceptional care for the elderly. Our founder, Bile Gebre, has over 20 years of experience in the healthcare industry and a deep passion for helping others. We are dedicated to providing a safe, comfortable, and loving environment for our residents. Our team of caregivers is highly trained and committed to providing the best care possible. We offer a wide range of services to meet the individual needs of each resident. Our goal is to help our residents live a happy, healthy, and fulfilling life. We invite you to visit our home and see for yourself the difference that Jona Adult Family Home can make in the life of your loved one.
